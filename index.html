About Me

Hi, I'm Adeena! I started crocheting when I was 8 years old, and now at 10, it's something I do almost every day! I love how fun and calming it is to take yarn and turn it into something amazing. My favorite things to make are crochet toys because they are cute, colourful and full of personality. I usually watch videos to help me learn new patterns and techniques. I've learned so much since I started, and I'm always excited to try something new and improve my skills. Thanks for visiting my website. I hope my crochet creations brng a smile to your face!
